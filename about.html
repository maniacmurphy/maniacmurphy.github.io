About me

Currently I work on infrastructure automation for VMware vCloud Air. I have always had the pleasure of working with individuals more experienced and more knowledgable than me. This tends to make me cranky because it serves as a constant reminder of how much more I have to learn. Luckily those more experienced individuals are also more patient and so I continue to have constant sources of differing experiences and points of view to keep me going.

About the blog

My initial focus is to help bring examples and understanding on how you can utilize the open source vmware puppet modules that I help work with on a regular basis. Over time I may grow into some ancillary post on other automation tools, but for now it will be all around puppet and the vmware modules

About layout

I like to use code to solve problems. I hate designing UI layouts which is why I am happy I was introduced to Jekyll for maintaining this blog and to Scott Emmons for creating the jekyll-clean theme. I have made a few modifications but the overall bulk of the work goes to Scott and Jekyll
